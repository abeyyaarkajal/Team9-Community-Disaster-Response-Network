Disaster Management Phases Coverage

CDRN aligns with official disaster management phases with weighted priorities.

Mitigation (20%)

Risk identification, hazard and vulnerability mapping, community awareness.

  • Hazard maps: flood, landslide, cyclone paths
  • Community vulnerability index
  • Safety guides and knowledge hub

Preparedness (25%)

Early warnings, training, checklists, volunteer readiness.

  • Real-time warnings from weather & IMD feeds
  • Training modules & mock drills
  • Family emergency checklist generator
  • Volunteer skill & readiness registry

Response (40%)

Real-time reporting, SOS, coordination, and resource routing.

  • Live incident reporting + SOS
  • Volunteer tasking & routing
  • Shelter & resource locator
  • Heatmaps and live map of roadblocks, rescues

Recovery (15%)

Damage assessment, compensation workflows, rehab tracking.

  • Damage assessment forms
  • Relief distribution dashboards
  • Case status tracking for affected families

Technical Architecture (Frontend View)

Frontend Responsibilities

  • Role-based views: Citizens, Volunteers, Authorities, NGOs
  • Forms for incidents, SOS, tasks, damage reports
  • Responsive layouts for mobile and desktop
  • Placeholder hooks for real-time WebSocket data
  • Map container ready for Mapbox / OSM integration

Backend Integration Points (Future)

  • /api/incidents – create + list incidents
  • /api/tasks – volunteer task assignments
  • /api/sos – high-priority alerts
  • /api/damage-reports – recovery workflows
  • WebSocket channels for live updates
